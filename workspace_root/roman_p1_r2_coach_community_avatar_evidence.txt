     4	 *
     5	 * OPERATOR RULE: Roman's voice is never disembodied. Every empty state that

     7	 * immediately knows it is Roman speaking — not generic app copy. This component
     8	 * is the single enforcement point: it always renders `<RomanAvatar />` above a

    11	 * Payload source of truth (fixer R1, face+voice contract): the `text` and

    13	 * (`GET /community/coach/empty-states`, surfaced via `useCoachEmptyStates`).
    14	 * The screen passes the resolved `RomanCopyPayload` straight through here, so
    15	 * the copy and crop are NEVER hardcoded at the call site — they are whatever
    16	 * the backend policy says they are. On a network/5xx error the screen renders
    17	 * `CoachErrorState` instead (honest error copy), never this celebratory/calm
    18	 * empty state, so an error can never masquerade as "all clear".
